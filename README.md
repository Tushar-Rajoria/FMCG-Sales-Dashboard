# 📊 FMCG Sales Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard built to analyze FMCG sales performance from **2022–2024**. This project helps business stakeholders monitor key sales metrics, evaluate product category performance, analyze stock availability, and identify sales trends across multiple sales channels.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this project is to transform raw FMCG sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations. The dashboard enables users to monitor KPIs, compare category performance, analyze stock levels, and explore sales trends over time.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Compare product category sales
- Analyze stock availability
- Track Month-to-Date (MTD) and Year-to-Date (YTD) sales
- Compare sales across different channels
- Identify high-performing product categories
- Support data-driven inventory and sales decisions

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Units Sold
- Total Stock Available
- Month-to-Date (MTD) Sales
- Year-to-Date (YTD) Sales

### Interactive Visualizations

- Product Category Performance
- Channel-wise Delivered Quantity
- Quarterly Sales Trend
- Quarterly Stock Trend
- Stock Distribution by Category
- Units Sold Distribution

### Interactive Filters

- Product Category
- Sales Channel
- Date Range
- Stock Date Range
- Highlight Category
- Reset Filters Button

## 📊 Key Insights

- Compare sales performance across different FMCG categories.

- Analyze quarterly sales and stock trends.
- Identify top-performing product categories.
- Evaluate channel-wise product delivery performance.
